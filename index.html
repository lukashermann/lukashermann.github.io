Biography

My name is Lukas Hermann and I am a computer scientist specialized in the area of machine learning and robotics. I graduated from the University of Freiburg in 2019 with a M.Sc. in computer science and got a position as a PhD Candidate in Prof. Dr. Wolfram Burgards Lab. Although academically fruitful with various publications, I took the hard decision to leave the PhD after 2 years. I came to the realization that, rather than solving abstract research questions, I would like to focus on finding innovative solutions for problems that have a more immediate impact.
